Window Tinting

Window Tinting for all makes and models.

At Sound Proof Custom Car Audio, we offer all types of window tinting solutions, for all types of vehicles. Ceramic film is also optional.

Look Sharp: Choose from an array of optically clear and tinted films and several levels of protection to get just the look and sun protection you desire. From basic and high- performance dyed films to metal combinations and top-performing all-metal films, LLumar will fit your style just right and dramatically enhance the appearance of any vehicle. LLumar even keeps the inside of your car in top form - reducing the fading and cracking that comes with unprotected sun exposure over time.

Ride Safe: Nothing is worse than dangerous glare. LLumar reduces glare and gives you a clearer, more comfortable view of the road ahead. Also, in the event of an accident, LLumar’s sturdy construction can help hold shattered glass in place and reduce injuries from flying glass. Stay Cool: With LLumar’s High Performance Resin (HPR) adhesive, the film smoothly hugs your vehicle - no bubbling or cracking.
